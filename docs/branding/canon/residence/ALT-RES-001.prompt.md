### Prompt

Create a wide, photorealistic cinematic interior of a modern mountain home before sunrise.

Show a calm, organized kitchen and living space with warm interior lighting against a cool blue dawn visible through large architectural windows. Outside, a misty mountain valley and distant peaks should remain quiet and still.

In the foreground, place an uncluttered preparation surface with coffee, an open notebook, a laptop showing a generic schedule interface without readable company branding, truck keys, and other quiet tools of readiness. Nearby, work boots should suggest that someone is about to begin the day.

No people should be visible. Presence must be implied only through the environment.

The scene should communicate quiet discipline, craftsmanship, preparation, and the authentic rhythm of a residential trades business owner—not luxury staging, product marketing, or software promotion.

Use eye-level architectural-editorial photography, realistic proportions, restrained color, soft shadows, and believable mixed lighting: warm interior glow against cool pre-dawn exterior light.

### Negative Direction

Avoid:

- visible people
- visible product placement
- visible company branding or logos
- readable Altair marks
- luxury spectacle or flashy wealth
- cluttered counters
- neon or RGB lighting
- blue interior LED lighting
- cinematic orange-and-teal grading
- heavy HDR
- excessive sharpness
- oversaturation
- stock-photo staging
- influencer aesthetics
- futuristic interfaces
- watermarks, captions, or overlaid marketing text
