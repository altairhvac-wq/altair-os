## Purpose

Establish the quiet morning rhythm of a residential service-business owner before the workday begins.

This image serves as the first residence-level Canon candidate after the world-establishing shot, showing preparation, craftsmanship, and calm discipline inside a modern mountain home.

## Story Summary

The home is still. Dawn has not fully arrived.

Warm interior light holds the kitchen while cool blue morning light fills the valley outside. Coffee steams beside an open notebook. A laptop shows a simple schedule. Truck keys, a tape measure, and work boots wait near the door.

No people are visible. Presence is implied through the environment—organized, lived-in, ready.

The image is about the rhythm of a trades business owner: quiet discipline, preparation before reaction, and a calm start to meaningful work. It is not luxury staging or product marketing.

## Creative Intent

The image should communicate:

- quiet discipline
- calm before the workday
- craftsmanship
- preparation
- mountain home
- warm interior against cool dawn outside
- coffee, notebook, work boots, and truck keys as tools of readiness
- an organized workspace without clutter
- presence implied through environment, with no people visible

The visual story should feel authentic and earned—not aspirational wealth, not software advertising, and not lifestyle product placement.

## Current Strengths

- Strong warm-interior / cool-dawn contrast
- Clear narrative of preparation without showing a person
- Believable lived-in details (coffee, notebook, boots, keys)
- Uncluttered composition with a single command-surface focus
- Mountain residence context that connects to the Altair world
- Emotional tone of calm discipline rather than urgency or luxury spectacle
