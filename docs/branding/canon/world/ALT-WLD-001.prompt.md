### Prompt

Create a wide, photorealistic cinematic landscape at first light in the American mountain West.

Show a misty mountain valley with a winding road moving through native grasses, rocks, mature pine trees, and a calm river. Mountains should rise naturally in the distance beneath a soft blue pre-dawn sky transitioning into warm sunrise light.

Include one modern mountain residence in the distance, built from natural stone, warm wood, matte dark metal, and architectural glass. Its warm interior lighting should remain faintly visible.

Include a clean professional service vehicle traveling quietly along the road. The vehicle should suggest that someone has already begun a purposeful workday, but it must remain visually secondary to the landscape.

The scene should communicate calm, discipline, preparedness, craftsmanship, quiet confidence, belonging, and possibility.

Use eye-level 35mm architectural-editorial photography, realistic proportions, natural atmospheric depth, straight verticals, restrained color, soft shadows, and believable dawn lighting.

The image should feel timeless, grounded, and aspirational without presenting wealth as spectacle.

### Negative Direction

Avoid:

- text or monument signs
- visible Altair logos
- dense luxury developments
- multiple prominent residences
- a work truck dominating the frame
- exotic vehicles
- neon or RGB lighting
- cinematic orange-and-teal grading
- heavy HDR
- excessive sharpness
- oversaturation
- futuristic architecture
- impossible mountains or roads
- stock-photo styling
- artificial-looking vegetation
- flashy wealth
- private jets or mega-yachts
- people posing for the camera
