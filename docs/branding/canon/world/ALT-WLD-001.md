## Purpose

Establish the broader world in which the Altair story takes place before introducing the founder, technicians, customers, residence interiors, or software.

This image serves as an environmental North Star for future Altair creative work.

## Story

The mountain valley is beginning to wake.

Morning light moves across the landscape while mist remains over the river and trees. A work vehicle is already traveling through the valley, suggesting that someone has begun the day with purpose.

The environment communicates that meaningful work, community, nature, and a well-built life can exist together.

## Creative Intent

The image should establish place rather than advertise a product.

It should communicate:

- calm
- discipline
- preparedness
- craftsmanship
- quiet confidence
- possibility
- belonging
- earned freedom

The visual story should feel aspirational without becoming a display of wealth.

## Current Strengths

- Strong sense of place
- Effective warm-versus-cool dawn lighting
- Natural mountain environment
- Clear visual depth
- Calm cinematic composition
- Work vehicle connects the landscape to the service-business story
- Architecture and nature feel part of one world

## Required Refinements

Before Canon approval:

1. Remove the stone monument or sign displaying “ALTAIR.”
2. Reduce the number of visible residences so the valley feels less like a planned luxury development.
3. Make the work vehicle smaller, farther down the road, and visually secondary to the landscape.
4. Preserve the river, mist, mountain depth, warm dawn light, native landscape, and winding-road composition.
5. Ensure the final image remains believable and avoids exaggerated luxury.
